Pillars of "Bangladesh"

Innovation

Innovation in Bangladesh has been growing steadily, driven by advancements in technology, entrepreneurship, and government support for digital transformation.

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh is increasingly being prioritized, especially in areas such as renewable energy, waste management, and sustainable agriculture.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure in Bangladesh has seen significant improvements in recent years, particularly in transportation, energy, and urban development.

Empowerment

Empowerment in Bangladesh has made considerable progress, especially in advancing gender equality and women's participation in various sectors

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Date: 12/05/2018   Time: 12:14 AM

Bangladesh's foray into space began with the launch of its first geostationary communications satellite, Bangabandhu-1, on May 12, 2018. The satellite was launched from the Kennedy Space Center in the USA aboard a SpaceX Falcon 9 Block 5 rocket, marking Bangladesh as the 57th country to operate a satellite in space.
